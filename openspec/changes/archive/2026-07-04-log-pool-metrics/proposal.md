## Why

The app currently presents pool chemistry as static sample content, but it does not provide a way to capture real readings over time. Adding structured logging for key water measurements will give users a practical record of their pool conditions and lay the groundwork for future trend review and maintenance insights.

## What Changes

- Add a pool log experience for recording pH, chlorine PPM, salt PPM, temperature, and total alkalinity.
- Store each reading locally so it remains available after reloads and future visits.
- Show recent entries in the app so users can review their latest test results quickly.

## Capabilities

### New Capabilities
- pool-metrics-logging: capture and retain pool water measurements for routine testing.

### Modified Capabilities
- None.

## Impact

- User interface for data entry and recent history review.
- Local persistence for pool measurement records.
- Future expansion for trend charts or maintenance reminders.
